About Me

My name is Ian Ashton and I am studying data science at Saint Paul College. Nine years after graduating from the University of Minnesota with a B.S. in Recreation, Park and Leisure Studies, I decided to head back to school to potentially turn what is a hobby for data and statistics into a new career.

So far I have learned Python and R and I am currently learning SQL and HTML. Coming up I will be taking a deeper dive into statistics where I will be learning different types of regression, ANOVA and data annalysis.

My interest in data and statistics is centered around sports. I would like to work for a professional sports team either in their data analysis department.

In my spare time, I have a huge passion for electronic music. I am very involved in the local Minneapolis scene and I also dj myself. I love house and techno specifically.

I also love parks and hiking, hence my original degree. I would like to visit every lower 48 National Park and do as much hiking as possible in each.
